Winterset Poker
Tourney on August, 13th.

Time: Tournaments are more structured then normal games, and so it is important to be there by 1:45 so we can start at 2:00. Please keep in mind Brady lives in the middle of no where so leaving at 1:30 is a good idea. The tournament will start at 2:00 PM or when 6 people are in play.
Also, a thirty-second time limit for any action can be enforced on a player by another player at the table by calling "Time" so that the number of hands per blind round is not unjustly shortened.

Buy-In: Your $10 Buy in gets you $1000 of tournament chips. Seating will be assigned randomly.


Re-Buys: If you lose your stack of chips before 8:00 PM, you may Re-Buy (Tournament speak for buy back in). Again, $10 gets you $1000 in chips. Rebuys will NOT be allowed after 8:00 PM. There will be no exceptions.


Payout: The top three or four players at the end of the game will be paid according to a percentage of the entire pot. The tournament will last until one person has all the chips. The payouts are as follows:
